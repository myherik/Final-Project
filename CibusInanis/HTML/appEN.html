The App


We have an app!


We at Cibus Inanis have now created an app that is meant to improve your shopping experience at our store. The app collects information about what you buy and then gives you personal discounts that fits your needs. It also tells you how much packaging and emissions you have spared the planet for. The more you buy at our shop and the more packaging you return, the more points you will receive in the app. When you collect points in our app, you will get a bronze, silver or gold membership based on the amount of points you have. The better the membership, the better the discounts you will get.
With our app you can:


  • See how much packaging you have spared the world for
  • Get discounts that is customized to you
  • Depending on how much you buy, you will get an upgraded membership with greater deals
  • See how much emissions you have spared the world for by buying local products
  • See which products are currently in season
